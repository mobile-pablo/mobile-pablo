Account Deletion Instructions

At Swipr, we respect your right to delete your account and associated data at any time. This page explains how to request account deletion and what data will be removed.

How to Delete Your Account

You can request account deletion in two ways:

Option 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open the Swipr app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Navigate to Account Settings
  4. Select Delete Account
  5. Confirm your decision

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to wsparcie.scriva@gmail.com with:

  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Your registered email address
  • Any additional account identifiers (username, if applicable)

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account profile and credentials
  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Documents and files stored on our servers
  • Settings and preferences
  • Usage history and analytics data
  • Any cloud-synced data associated with your account
Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please ensure you have backed up any important documents before proceeding.

Data Retention

After you request account deletion:

  • Your account will be deactivated immediately
  • Most data will be deleted within 30 days
  • Some data may be retained for legal or regulatory purposes for up to 90 days
  • Anonymized usage data may be retained for analytical purposes

Local Data

Please note that deleting your account will not automatically remove data stored locally on your device. To remove local data:

  1. Go to your device Settings
  2. Find Swipr in your app list
  3. Select "Clear Data" or "Clear Storage"
  4. Alternatively, uninstall the app

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within:

  • In-app requests: Immediate to 24 hours
  • Email requests: 2-5 business days
